TipFatigue.org

A Movement Against Exploitative Tipping Culture

The Problem

Tipping culture has spiraled out of control. Businesses now demand tips before service is provided, creating awkward pressure and shifting wage responsibility from employers to customers.

The Solution

Rate businesses honestly, but subtract one star for tip-before-service practices. Include "TipFatigue.org" in your review to raise awareness and push back against exploitative tipping culture.

How to Participate

  1. Rate the business honestly based on service and product quality
  2. Subtract one star if they request tips before service
  3. Mention "TipFatigue.org" in your review

Sample Review

"Great coffee and friendly staff (would be 5 stars), but deducting 1 star for the tip prompt before service. Join TipFatigue.org to push back against exploitative tipping culture."

Join the Movement

Share TipFatigue.org and help restore tipping to what it should be: voluntary appreciation for exceptional service.
