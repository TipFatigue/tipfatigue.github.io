TipFatigue.org

A Movement Against Exploitative Tipping Culture

The Problem

Tipping culture has spiraled out of control. What once was a gesture of appreciation for exceptional service has become an expectation before any service is even provided. From coffee shops to takeout counters, customers are increasingly pressured to tip upfront, often through intimidating payment screens with preset percentages.

This practice shifts the burden of fair wages from employers to customers, creating awkward social pressure and normalizing below-living wages for workers who deserve better.

The Solution

TipFatigue.org is a grassroots movement empowering consumers to push back against exploitative tipping practices through their reviews and ratings.

Our approach is simple and effective: Rate businesses honestly for their product and service quality, but when they engage in problematic tipping practices, subtract one star and mention the TipFatigue.org movement in your review.

How to Participate

  1. Experience the service: Visit the business and note their tipping practices
  2. Rate honestly: Give your genuine rating based on service and product quality
  3. Subtract for bad tipping practices: If they engage in problematic tipping, reduce your rating by one star
  4. Mention the movement: Include "TipFatigue.org" in your review to raise awareness
  5. Be respectful: Focus on the policy, not individual workers who don't set these rules

Sample Review Language

"The coffee was excellent and staff was friendly (would be 5 stars), but I'm deducting 1 star for the aggressive tip prompt before any service was provided. Join the TipFatigue.org movement to push back against exploitative tipping culture that hurts both customers and workers."

Join the Movement

Every review counts. When businesses see consistent feedback about their tipping practices affecting their ratings, they'll be motivated to change. Together, we can restore tipping to what it should be: a voluntary gesture of appreciation for exceptional service.
